## What Is GuacGG Swap

**GuacGG Swap** ([guacgg-swap.com](https://guacgg-swap.com)) is a **Solana-native decentralized exchange** that combines serious DeFi infrastructure with a community-first, gamified experience. The platform delivers sub-second token swaps, deep liquidity aggregation, and a suite of engagement features — trading competitions, leaderboards, achievement systems, and reward distributions — that make trading on Solana more fun and more rewarding.

GuacGG isn't just another DEX fork. It's a trading community with its own culture, rewards system, and ongoing events.

---

## Key Features

### Sub-Second Solana Swaps
Built on Solana's 400ms block time. Every swap settles in under a second. No pending transactions, no congestion delays.

### Gamified Trading Rewards
Earn points, climb leaderboards, complete challenges, and receive token rewards for active trading. The more you participate, the more you earn.

### Deep Liquidity Aggregation
Liquidity sourced from Raydium, Orca, Meteora, and GuacGG's native pools. Smart routing ensures best execution on every trade.

### Full SPL Token Coverage
Trade all popular Solana tokens — SOL, BONK, WIF, JUP, USDC, USDT, RAY, ORCA, and hundreds of memecoins and DeFi tokens.

### Community Events
Regular trading competitions, AMAs, token launches, and community initiatives. Active participation is rewarded.

### Non-Custodial
No custody of user funds. Connect your Solana wallet, swap, and your tokens stay in your control at all times.

## Liquidity and Routing

GuacGG's routing engine aggregates from:

- **Raydium** — AMM and CLMM pools
- **Orca** — Whirlpools and concentrated liquidity
- **Meteora** — Dynamic vaults and DLMM
- **GuacGG Native Pools** — Protocol-owned liquidity

The router automatically splits orders across sources when splitting produces better execution.
